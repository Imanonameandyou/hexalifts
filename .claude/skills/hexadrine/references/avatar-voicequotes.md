# Avatar Voice Quotes Bank

Verbatim quotes from real Hexadrine avatars, organized by emotional state.
Load this file when writing copy that needs to sound like it came from inside the reader's head.

## Gym Fear & Social Embarrassment

- *"I don't go because I'm scared — extremely muscular tall guys, I'm afraid to talk to them because I'm just a skinny nerd."*
- *"I feel like everyone is judging how weak I am."*
- *"I just feel very embarrassed... everyone else looks strong and working out compared to me being really fat and weak. Everyone else is doing well with weights... makes me want to quit."*
- *"I want to look better for graduation and college, but everyone else is so strong... I just feel like a piece of shit."*
- *"Because of my height I look really fat... I just feel like a piece of shit."*

## The Start-Stop Cycle

- *"At first I worked out... I reach the point I'm looking how I want, but then the incentive is no longer strong enough... I end up reducing frequency and slim back down... then realize I want to be big again. Rinse and repeat."*
- *"I stick to a routine for 30 days, but after that 30 days was up, I find it hard sticking to it."*
- *"I want to be muscular, so I work out... but once I start looking how I want, the incentive is no longer strong enough."*
